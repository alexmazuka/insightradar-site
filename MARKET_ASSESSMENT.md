# InsightRadar — Market Assessment & Sales Strategy

## 1. Product Summary

InsightRadar Signals UA — AI-powered media intelligence service delivering daily Western media digests in Ukrainian via Telegram. Current metrics: 3,200+ subscribers, 12 daily posts, 20+ Western media sources.

**Subscription tiers:**
- Free: $0 (public channel, 3 digests/day)
- Pro: $9/mo (full access, weekly outlook, email)
- Premium: $29/mo (custom filters, API, analyst access)

## 2. TAM / SAM / SOM (Ukrainian Market)

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
Media intelligence & monitoring in Ukraine and related markets:
- ~15,000 organizations in Ukraine that consume analytical content (government, NGOs, media, business)
- ~50,000 individual professionals (analysts, journalists, diplomats, PR)
- **TAM: ~$3.9M/year** (65,000 potential users x avg $60/year)

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
Ukrainian-speaking professionals actively seeking Western media analysis:
- ~5,000 organizations with international exposure
- ~15,000 individuals active on Telegram
- **SAM: ~$1.2M/year** (20,000 x avg $60/year)

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) — Year 1-2
Realistic conversion from current base + outreach:
- 10,000 free subscribers (from current 3,200)
- 500 Pro subscribers ($9/mo = $54K/year)
- 50 Premium subscribers ($29/mo = $17.4K/year)
- **SOM: ~$71.4K/year** ($5,950/month)

- Companies: Ukrinform, Interfax-Ukraine, Ukrainian media groups, PR agencies
- LinkedIn Navigator: ~2,000-3,000 profiles

**Persona 4: Business (International-facing)**
- Title filters: CEO, COO, Strategy Director, Business Development, Government Relations
- Companies: Ukrainian IT companies, agri-export, energy sector
- LinkedIn Navigator: ~3,000-5,000 profiles
- Approach: Premium subscription for strategic intelligence

**Week 1-2: Connection + Awareness**
- Connect with 50-100 profiles/week per persona

**Week 3-4: Content + Nurture**
- Share 2-3 insightful posts/week from InsightRadar digests
- Engage with prospects' content

**Week 5-6: Conversion**
- Direct message with free trial invitation
- Case study or sample digest PDF

**Expected conversion funnel:**
- Connections sent: 200/week

- Engaged: 18 (30% of accepted)
- Free trial: 9 (50% of engaged)
- Paid conversion: 2-3 (25-33% of trial)

## 6. SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- First mover in Ukrainian AI media intelligence niche
- Low operational costs ($150-270/month)
- High margin (90%+ at scale)
- Strong product-market fit (validated by 3,200 organic subscribers)
- Telegram as delivery = zero acquisition cost for free tier

### Weaknesses
- Single developer dependency
- No brand recognition beyond current audience
- No payment infrastructure yet (LiqPay/Stripe needed)
- Content quality dependent on AI prompt engineering

### Opportunities
- Ukraine's growing need for Western media monitoring (war context)
- B2B/institutional sales (embassies, NGOs, government)
- Expansion to other post-Soviet markets (Georgia, Moldova, Baltic)
- API monetization for media companies
- Partnership with think tanks and universities

### Threats
- OpenAI API cost increases
- Competitors entering the niche (Molfar, VoxUkraine adding similar features)
- Telegram platform risk (regulation, blocks)
- War fatigue reducing Western media coverage of Ukraine
- Currency risk (UAH volatility affects purchasing power)
