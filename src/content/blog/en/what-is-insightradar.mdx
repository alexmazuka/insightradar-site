## Why InsightRadar Exists

In today's information environment, thousands of analytical articles are published daily by leading Western outlets. CNN, BBC, Reuters, The Guardian, AP News — each generates dozens of articles directly relevant to Ukraine, its security, and its future.

The problem is that manually tracking this flow is impossible. Even an experienced analyst needs several hours daily just to scan the headlines of major outlets.

## How InsightRadar Works

InsightRadar is an AI-powered monitoring system that delivers a ready PDF digest to your Telegram every day by 08:00 Kyiv time:

- Collecting articles from 20+ verified Western media sources via RSS
- AI analysis and creation of structured summaries in Ukrainian
- Generating a PDF digest with key points and context for Ukraine
- Delivery to Telegram every morning by 08:00

Each digest contains an analytical summary of key publications from the past 24 hours, focused on topics relevant to Ukraine.

InsightRadar is built for those who make decisions based on understanding the external media environment: analysts, diplomats, journalists, business leaders, government officials, and anyone who wants to understand what the Western world thinks and writes about Ukraine.
