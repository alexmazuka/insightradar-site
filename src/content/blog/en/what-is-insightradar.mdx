## Why InsightRadar Exists

In today's information environment, thousands of analytical articles are published daily by leading Western outlets. CNN, BBC, Reuters, The Guardian, AP News — each generates dozens of articles directly relevant to Ukraine, its security, and its future.

The problem is that manually tracking this flow is impossible. Even an experienced analyst needs several hours daily just to scan the headlines of major outlets.

## How InsightRadar Works

InsightRadar uses artificial intelligence to automate three key processes:

- Collecting articles from 20+ verified sources via RSS feeds
- AI analysis and creation of structured summaries in Ukrainian
- Delivering ready digests via Telegram 12 times per day

Each digest contains key points of the article, context for Ukraine, and a link to the original.

## Who It's For

InsightRadar is built for those who make decisions based on understanding the external media environment: analysts, diplomats, journalists, business leaders, and anyone who wants to understand what the Western world thinks and writes about Ukraine.
