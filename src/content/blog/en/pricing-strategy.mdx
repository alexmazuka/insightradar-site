## The Cost of Understanding Western Media

For Ukrainian organizations operating in an international context, Western media monitoring is not a luxury — it's a working tool. But how much should it cost?

## Option 1: Staff Analyst

Hiring an analyst for daily Western media monitoring costs 15,000-30,000 UAH per month. One person physically cannot cover more than 5-10 sources per working day. There are also risks: illness, vacation, burnout — and you're left without monitoring.

## Option 2: Enterprise Platform

Meltwater, Cision, Brandwatch — powerful media monitoring systems costing 20,000-60,000 UAH per month. Additionally, you need someone to work with dashboards and interpret data. Ideal for large corporations, overkill for most Ukrainian organizations.

## Option 3: Google Alerts

Free, but no analytics, no translation, no structuring. You need to read each article in English yourself and draw conclusions. Time cost: 2-3 hours per day.
