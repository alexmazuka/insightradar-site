How AI Transforms Media Intelligence

An overview of how artificial intelligence technologies are changing approaches to monitoring and analyzing the media landscape.

AItechnology

## A New Era of Media Intelligence

Artificial intelligence has fundamentally changed the capabilities of media landscape analysis. What previously required a team of 10-15 analysts can now be done by a single AI system with minimal human oversight.

Key AI Capabilities in Media Intelligence

Modern language models enable:

  • Automatic translation preserving context and nuances
  • Sentiment and tone detection in text
  • Classification by topics and priorities
  • Generation of structured summaries

How InsightRadar Uses AI

Our system uses OpenAI GPT to process each article in several stages. First, the relevance of the material is determined, then a structured summary is created in Ukrainian, highlighting key points and context for Ukraine.

Quality vs Automation

The main challenge is balancing speed and quality. We constantly refine our prompts and filtering algorithms to ensure high analytical quality with full process automation. Our goal is 90%+ accuracy in extracting key points.

The Future

We plan to integrate sentiment analysis, automatic trend detection, and custom filters for each user. AI-powered intelligence is becoming accessible to everyone.
