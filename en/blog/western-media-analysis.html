## External Perception Matters

How Ukraine is portrayed in Western media directly influences politicians' decisions, aid volumes, and public support in partner countries. Understanding this narrative is not a luxury but a strategic necessity.

What Systematic Monitoring Provides

InsightRadar's daily digest, delivered by 08:00 to Telegram as a PDF, enables you to:

  • Start your workday with an understanding of key publications from the past 24 hours
  • Track changes in rhetoric of key outlets
  • Predict shifts in partner countries' policies
  • Identify new narratives and trends at early stages
  • Understand which arguments resonate with Western audiences

Challenges of Manual Monitoring

The main issue is scale. Daily, the top 20 Western outlets publish 500+ articles, of which 30-50 may be relevant to Ukraine. Reviewing, translating, and analyzing this volume requires an entire team of analysts and costs from 15,000 to 30,000 UAH per month for a single staff specialist.
